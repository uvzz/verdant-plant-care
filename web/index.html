Good questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need an account to use Verdant?＋

No. Verdant is local-first — add plants, log care, and take progress photos entirely offline with no account. Signing in with Apple or Google is optional and only turns on cloud backup and cross-device sync.

What makes “check-before-water” different?＋

Most apps water on a fixed timer, which is the #1 cause of houseplant death. Verdant nudges you to feel the soil first; if it’s still damp, one tap snoozes the reminder. Intervals also adapt to each plant’s pot size and light.

Where does the AI run — is my data safe?＋

AI identify and coaching run on Verdant’s own servers. The API key is never bundled in the app or placed on your device, so there’s nothing to leak. Photos stay on your phone unless you explicitly enable sync.

Is there a free plan or just a trial?＋

A genuine free plan: up to 5 plants with the full check-before-water calendar, progress photos, insights, and family sharing — no time limit. Premium adds unlimited plants, AI, and cloud sync.

Which platforms are supported?＋

Verdant is built for iOS and Android. Sign in with Apple on iPhone or Google on any device to sync your collection across them.

How does cloud sync work across devices?＋

Sign in once and your plants, care history, and photos back up automatically and appear on every device signed into the same account. It’s optional, encrypted in transit, and off until you turn it on.
