UX notes

  • Photo hierarchy — every primary screen leads with imagery; forms never bury the portrait.
  • Specimen strip — name (Fraunces) + italic species + due filament replaces generic badges.
  • Copper is rare — only overdue states, so urgency stays legible.
  • Growth chartreuse — primary actions and “due today” share one optimistic signal.
  • Empty copy — “Your glasshouse is quiet” invites action without guilt.
  • Modals — Add / Log Care use calm sheets; Cancel is always top-left, primary bottom-full.
  • Open mockups: design/screens.html · tokens: design/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md
